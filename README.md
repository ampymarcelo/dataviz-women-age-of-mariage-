# 💍 Mean Age of Women at First Marriage (2020)

> Visualizing early marriage patterns worldwide to support  
> gender equality advocacy and evidence-based programming.

---

## 🌍 Why This Project?

Child and early marriage remains one of the most critical barriers  
to gender equality and women's empowerment globally.

This project transforms **raw UN population data into a clear, readable chart**  
showing which countries have the lowest mean age at first marriage —  
with a focus on the **Age 18 threshold** recommended by international standards.

Built for professionals at **UNFPA, UNICEF, UN Women**, and anyone  
working on gender data and reproductive health programming.

---

## 📊 What This Visualization Shows

A horizontal bar chart of the **Top 15 countries** ranked by mean age  
of women at first marriage in **2020**, featuring :

- 🔴 Countries ranked from highest to lowest mean age
- 🔵 A clear **Age 18 threshold line** to identify at-risk countries
- 📌 Focus on countries where women marry **before or just at age 18**

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- 📉 Several countries in the dataset show a mean age **below 18 years**
- 🌍 Sub-Saharan African and South Asian countries dominate the lower end
- 🔵 The Age 18 threshold line reveals how many countries **fall short**  
  of international child marriage standards
- 📅 2020 data captures marriage patterns during the **COVID-19 period**,  
  known to have worsened early marriage rates globally
